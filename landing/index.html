Notion - Reducing noise

Amplifying information through intelligence.

Notion allows you to get context about any incident or news through our intelligent platform that creates a timeline of events in any news so that you get the context of any information you consume.

We empower communities to curate content together so that you get to decide what the world consumes as a community and reduce the problem of fake news.

We allow you to cite the entire timeline of events as source so that you can refer to them in different media forms.

Curate

Curate your own decks and share them with the world.

We allow users to share their opinion with the world by curating existing decks or creating new ones. Curation may involve :

  • Add comments about specific lines of existing decks.

  • Insert media like tweets and videos to refer to something.

  • Use pre fetched decks and add your own cards to it.

  • Refer to your curated deck in various media sources.
